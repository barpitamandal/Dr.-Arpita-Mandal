Research Interests

Advanced mathematical modeling of fluid boundary layer phenomena in various engineering applications

Heat & Mass Transfer

Thermal analysis and mass transport mechanisms in complex fluid systems and porous media

Nanofluids & Hybrid Nanofluids

Investigation of enhanced thermal properties in nanoparticle-suspended fluid systems

Spectral Methods

Advanced numerical techniques including Spectral Quasi Linearization Method for complex problems

Biomedical Applications

Oxygen transfer modeling, membrane transport, and oxyhaemoglobin diffusion studies

About Me

Research Excellence in Mathematical Sciences

I am a dedicated mathematical research scientist currently serving as a Senior Research Assistant at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. My research focuses on the intricate world of fluid dynamics, particularly in magnetohydrodynamics boundary layer flows and nanofluid applications.

With a Ph.D. in Fluid Dynamics and Mathematics from NIT Jamshedpur, I specialize in advanced computational methods including Spectral Quasi Linearization Method (SQLM) and COMSOL Multiphysics simulations. My work bridges theoretical mathematics with practical engineering applications.
